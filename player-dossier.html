KNIVESOUT
PLAYER DOSSIER
OBJECTIVE
Eliminate your assigned target and survive. Someone else is hunting you at the same time.

The system forms a closed loop — you will never receive your own name, and every player is both hunter and target.
JOINING THE GAME
You need an account to play. Sign up with a codename and password before the game starts.

HOW TO JOIN:
1
Open the game link and tap SIGN UP
2
Enter your codename and a password you'll remember
3
Once the moderator starts the game, log back in — your target will be waiting
Your codename is your identity for the entire game. Choose carefully.
LOGGING BACK IN:
Use the LOGIN tab with the same codename and password you signed up with.
YOUR DASHBOARD
Your status and target are hidden by default to protect your intel if someone glances at your screen.

TO REVEAL YOUR INFO:
PRESS AND HOLD TO REVEAL INTEL

Press and hold the button — your status, target name, and any active kill codes appear while held. Release to hide immediately.
Never release in a public space. Your target's name is classified.
WHAT YOU'LL SEE:
Your current status (ALIVE / GHOST)
Your assigned target's name
Any active kill code you've generated
THE KILL CHAIN
Every player is part of a continuous loop.

When you eliminate your target:
You inherit their target
The chain closes behind you
The hunt continues

This repeats until only one player remains.
Eliminate. Inherit. Repeat.

KILL RULES
A kill that breaks any rule below is void. No exceptions.
1
Game is active everywhere.
Outside the office, at lunch, on a commute, at events — the game never pauses unless a specific exemption below applies.
2
Office floor: target must be standing.
On the office floor only, your target is protected while seated at their desk or in a chair. Wait until they're on their feet.
3
No kills in front of player eyewitnesses.
If any other active player can clearly see the kill happen, it is void. Non-players watching do not count.
4
Physical contact + verbal confirmation required.
You must make direct physical contact and explicitly say "you're dead" (or equivalent) before showing the code. Silent kills are void.
5
No kills during meetings or business duties.
If your target is in a meeting, on a call, presenting, or fulfilling a work duty, they are protected until the obligation ends.

EXAMPLES:
✓ VALID KILLS
You tap your target on the shoulder in the corridor, say "you're dead", and show them the code.
Your target is standing at the printer on the office floor. No other players nearby. Contact made, words said.
You catch your target outside the office at lunch — no meeting active, no player witnesses in sight.
Target is standing at the coffee machine. You approach, make contact, say the words, show code.
✗ VOID KILLS
Your target is seated at their desk on the office floor. You must wait until they stand up.
Another active player is nearby and clearly sees the kill happen. Void regardless of location.
Your target is on a client call. Even if you're right next to them, the kill is void until it ends.
You show the code without making physical contact or saying anything. Silent kills are not valid.
When in doubt, wait. A disputed kill helps no one — least of all you.

GHOST PROTOCOL
Death may grant one final opportunity.
When N players die, ghost system activates.

PHASE 1: Alive players vote → Ghost selected

PHASE 2: Dead players vote → Target assigned

OUTCOME:
SUCCESS → Return to game
FAIL → Permanent death

WHAT HAPPENS IF YOU'RE SELECTED AS GHOST:
Revived
You re-enter the game as a GHOST with a specific target assigned by the dead players' vote.
Your mission
Eliminate your assigned ghost target using the normal kill code system. Your target does not know you're hunting them.
If you succeed
You take their place in the chain — becoming fully alive again. The person previously hunting your victim now hunts you instead.
Time limit
You have a limited window (default 24 hours) shown as a live countdown on your dashboard. If it hits zero before you kill — permanent death.
If you fail
Time expires → status changes to DEAD permanently. No further chances.
As a ghost, use the same GENERATE CODE flow under "I AM KILLER" to make your kill.
