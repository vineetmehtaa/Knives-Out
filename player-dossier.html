KILL RULES
A kill that breaks any rule below is void. No exceptions.
1
Game is active everywhere.
Outside the office, at lunch, on a commute, at events — the game never pauses unless a specific exemption below applies.
2
Office floor: target must be standing.
On the office floor only, your target is protected while seated at their desk or in a chair. Wait until they're on their feet.
3
No kills in front of player eyewitnesses.
If any other active player can clearly see the kill happen, it is void. Non-players watching do not count.
4
Physical contact + verbal confirmation required.
You must make direct physical contact and explicitly say "you're dead" (or equivalent) before showing the code. Silent kills are void.
5
No kills during meetings or business duties.
If your target is in a meeting, on a call, presenting, or fulfilling a work duty, they are protected until the obligation ends.

When in doubt, wait. A disputed kill helps no one — least of all you.
🛡 IMMUNITY CLAIM
Limited slots. First come, first served. Your claim time is your race position.
WHAT IT IS

Once per day there is a short immunity claim window (typically 3:00–3:05 PM IST, five minutes). Alive players tap CLAIM IMMUNITY as fast as they can. Only a limited number of players win each day — the fastest valid claims take the slots.

If you win a slot, you are immune until 2:30 PM the next day IST (30 minutes before the next window). Immunity does not stack from one day to the next — each day is a new race.

YOUR DAY (PLAYER VIEW)
1
~2 min before open — IMMUNITY CLAIM appears with “get ready.” No button yet.
2
Window open — Tap CLAIM IMMUNITY once. Spamming does not help; your first valid claim time is what counts.
3
Window closes — Fastest claimants win immunity. Everyone else does not.
4
Until ~2:30 PM tomorrow — If you won, you keep immunity until it is used up in a kill (see below) or the day’s round ends.

WHAT IMMUNITY DOES IN A KILL

Immunity only matters when a valid kill code is entered against you. It does not stop meetings, desk rules, or eyewitness voids — those are separate kill rules.

1
First valid attack while immune — The kill does not count. You stay alive. Your immunity is used up (gone for the rest of this round).
2
Victim sees — A message that the attack was stopped and you no longer have immunity.
3
Killer sees — In the kill-code area (same styling as the game):
🛡 Immunity blocked your kill

Your victim was immune. They're no longer protected — try again with a new code.

🛡 You survived

The attack was consumed by your immunity. You're now vulnerable.

4
Second attack — After immunity is consumed, a new valid code confirms the kill normally.
One immunity = one block. It is not a permanent shield for the whole day after use.

👻 GHOST PROTOCOL
Death may grant one final opportunity — when revival voting opens in your game.

After enough eliminations, the app can open a two-stage vote. You will see a 👻 GHOST REVIVAL VOTE card on your dashboard when it is your turn to participate.

STAGE 1 — ALIVE VOTE (👻 VOTE TO REVIVE)
If you are alive and eligible, pick one dead or ghost player to bring back
After you vote, you see a confirmation and must wait until Stage 2 opens
STAGE 2 — DEAD VOTE (🎯 VOTE A TARGET FOR GHOST)
If you are dead and eligible, vote which alive player the ghost must assassinate
The revived player becomes a GHOST with that assignment
OUTCOME
SUCCESS → You return as GHOST, then ALIVE if you complete the kill in time
FAIL → Timer hits zero → status returns to DEAD permanently

IF YOU ARE THE REVIVED GHOST:
Revived
You re-enter the game as a GHOST with a specific target assigned by the dead players' vote.
Your mission
Eliminate your assigned ghost target using the normal kill code system. Your target does not know you're hunting them.
If you succeed
You take their place in the chain — becoming fully alive again. The person previously hunting your victim now hunts you instead.
Time limit
You have a limited window (default 24 hours) shown as a live countdown on your dashboard. If it hits zero before you kill — permanent death.
If you fail
Time expires → status changes to DEAD permanently. No further chances.
Use ⚔ CONFIRM A KILL → I AM KILLER → GENERATE KILL CODE. The code targets your ghost assignment, not your old chain target.
